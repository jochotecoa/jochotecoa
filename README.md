# Hi there, I'm Juan Ochoteco Asensio 👋

I am a **Postdoctoral Researcher in Cancer Epigenetics** at **CABIMER** (Andalusia Center for Molecular Biology and Regenerative Medicine). My work focuses on decoding complex multi-omics regulatory networks to understand disease mechanisms.

### 🔬 Research Focus
- **Epigenetics & Multi-Omics:** Integrating transcriptomics, proteomics, and epigenomic data (e.g., toxicogenomics and cancer research).
- **Circular RNA (circRNA):** Analyzing circRNA-miRNA-mRNA regulatory axes in cardiovascular and oncology contexts.
- **Machine Learning in Biology:** Developing predictive models to bridge different omics layers (e.g., predicting protein levels from mRNA).

### 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Languages:** R (Expert), Python, Bash, SQL.
- **Bioinformatics:** Bioconductor, DESeq2, Tidyverse, miRanda, biomaRt.
- **Data Science:** Caret (Machine Learning), ggplot2 (Visualization), Jupyter.
- **Reproducibility:** Renv, Git/GitHub, Modular Code Design.
